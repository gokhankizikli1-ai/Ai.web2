# Overview

KorvixAI is an AI Operating System currently under active development.

The objective of KorvixAI is **not** to become another chatbot.

Instead, KorvixAI is being designed as a complete AI Operating System capable of planning, reasoning, executing, collaborating and continuously improving through multiple specialized AI agents.

Every architectural decision should support this long-term vision.

---

# Mission

Our mission is to build the world's most capable AI Operating System.

KorvixAI should become the primary workspace where users can build businesses, software, websites, research, games, trading systems and automations through collaboration with intelligent AI agents.

---

# Vision

Long term, KorvixAI should replace many traditional software tools by combining them into one intelligent platform.

Instead of opening ten different applications, users should simply open KorvixAI.

Future capabilities include:

* Software Engineering
* Business Management
* Website Creation
* Shopify Store Builder
* Game Development
* Research
* Trading
* Marketing

# Development Philosophy

KorvixAI follows several engineering principles.

## Security First

Security is never optional.

Authentication, authorization, encryption and user isolation are mandatory.

---

## Production First

Every feature should be production-ready.

Avoid temporary hacks.

Avoid shortcuts.

Always think about maintainability.

---

## Modular Architecture

Every system should remain modular.

Components should be replaceable without affecting unrelated parts of the system.

---

## AI Native

KorvixAI is built around AI.

AI should not be treated as an additional feature.

AI is the foundation of every system.

---

## Multi-Agent Ready

Every new feature should be designed assuming multiple AI agents will collaborate together.

Avoid single-agent assumptions.

---
